Swimlane: Divides the diagram into sections to show who (actor/system) performs each action
Control Flow: Arrow indicating the sequence and direction of steps
Action / Activity: A single step or task in the workflow
Activity State: Another term for an action (a step in the process)
State: A condition or situation at a point in time
Initial State: The starting point of the workflow (filled circle)
Final State: The ending point of the workflow (bullseye symbol)
Decision Node: A branching point for conditional logic (if/else)
Merge: Combines multiple alternative paths into one
Fork: Splits one flow into multiple parallel flows
Join: Synchronizes parallel flows back into one
Guard: A condition label on a flow (e.g., \[valid], \[error])
